Hi, I’m Flynn - a political scientist and technologist working on frontier tech and the future of governance.

I’m Co-Director of Boundary Object Studio, where we work on frontier technology governance, human-centric design, and novel deployment of technology for public good.

I have been a startup founder (funded by Matt Clifford’s ‘def/acc’ programme), first hire at The Collective Intelligence Project, staff at the Alan Turing Institute’s Public Policy programme, co-initiator of the world’s first global citizens’ assembly, and advisor to the OECD.
